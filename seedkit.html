About SeedKit

Bringing fun, affordable, sustainable and equitable science experiments to the world! Our Mission Statement

We at SeedKit are dedicated to producing low-cost, sustainable, and engaging science experiments for use in low-resource classrooms around the world. Each kit comes with a set of experiment protocols for teacher and up to 24 students, as well as most of the materials necessary to complete the labs without electricity, running water, or difficult-to-obtain supplies.

Our first set of kits was created with the high schooler in mind, and contains fun lab activities in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. This summer, a couple of lucky SeedKit team members are going abroad to see the new prototypes in action and bring back feedback from teachers and students.
